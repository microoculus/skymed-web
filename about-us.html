About Us

Who We Are

Your Strategic Partner in Health & Pharma Investments

INFIKOR Holdings serves as the parent company to a group of pharmaceutical and healthcare companies operating across India, UAE, Ukraine, Uzbekistan, Malaysia, and the French West Africa region. Our journey began with the need for a central entity to support, invest in, and guide our existing high-performance companies.

Today, INFIKOR functions as a strategic growth engine that drives investment, fosters innovation, and enables operational synergy across our subsidiaries. Together, we aim to transform healthcare systems and improve access to world-class medical solutions.
