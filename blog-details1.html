INFIKOR Group to Launch SKY NITI Pharma in Kuala Lumpur as Regional Hub for Southeast Asia Expansion

INFIKOR Group of Companies is proud to announce the upcoming launch of its newest entity — SKY NITI Pharma SDN BHD, officially registered in the dynamic and fast-paced capital of Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur. This marks yet another significant milestone in our ongoing global expansion.

Positioned strategically in one of Southeast Asia’s most promising markets, SKY NITI Pharma will serve as a regional hub as we set our sights on expanding into Vietnam, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, the Philippines, and Thailand.

Its core focus areas include pharmaceutical sales and marketing, logistics, regulatory affairs, and licensing. The company also specializes in the sales and marketing of medical devices and nutraceuticals.

INFIKOR Holdings, headquartered in Dubai and established in 2022, is a visionary investment and strategic management group focused on advancing global healthcare.
