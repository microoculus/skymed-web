About INFIKOR

Your Strategic Partner in Health & Pharma Investments

INFIKOR Holdings, headquartered in Dubai and established in 2022, is a visionary investment and strategic management group focused on advancing global healthcare. Founded after the successful establishment of several high-performing pharmaceutical companies, INFIKOR was created to bring them together under a unified, strategic umbrella—ensuring they grow efficiently, collaboratively, and sustainably.
